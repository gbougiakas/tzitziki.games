Bandanas Privacy Policy

This Personal Data Protection Policy is fully compatible with the European Regulation on the protection of natural persons with regard to the processing of personal data and aims to inform you about the information collected from you during your visit to the video game Bandanas ("the Game").

In the following terms you will find important information regarding the type, purpose of use of your personal data, their protection, as well as your rights as subjects of this data.

For any clarification you can contact the email info@tzitziki.games

Effective Date: 11/07/2025

This privacy policy applies to the video game Bandanas ("the Game"), developed using Unity.

1. No Data Collection

The Game does not collect, store, or share any personal or non-personal data from users.

2. No Internet Connection

The Game operates entirely offline. It does not access the internet, use online services, or connect to any servers. Since the Game does not access the internet, no cookies are created or stored.

3. No Registration or Account Required

Users can play the Game without creating an account, signing in, or providing any information. For this reason, we consider it appropriate that you regularly refer to this Policy.

4. No Third-Party Services

The Game does not use any third-party services or SDKs that collect user data.

5. Apple App Store, Google Play Store, Unity

The Game does not collect any user data, however, Apple, Google or Unity might. Please refer to their Privacy Policies for more information.

Unity Privacy Policy: https://unity.com/legal/game-player-and-app-user-privacy-policy
Apple Privacy Policy: https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/en-ww/
Google Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

6. Children's Privacy

Since no data is collected, the Game is compliant with privacy regulations regarding children's data, such as COPPA and GDPR-K.

7. Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy changes in the future, the updated version will be made available along with the Game or on its official website.

8. Contact

If you have any questions about this policy, you can contact the developer at: https://tzitziki.games/support.html
